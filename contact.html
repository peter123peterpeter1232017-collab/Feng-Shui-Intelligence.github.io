Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and feng shui analysis

How accurate is your AI analysis?

Our AI has been trained on thousands of interior spaces and validated by certified Feng Shui masters. While it provides highly accurate general recommendations, we always suggest consulting with a professional for complex situations or major renovations.

What if I'm not satisfied with the results?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our plans. If you're not completely satisfied with your analysis, simply contact our support team for a full refund.

Do I need to make major changes to my home?

Not necessarily! Many Feng Shui improvements can be achieved through simple adjustments like rearranging furniture, adding plants, or changing colors. Our AI analysis will prioritize recommendations based on impact and ease of implementation.

Can I use this for commercial spaces?

Yes! Our Professional and Enterprise plans include analysis for commercial spaces like offices, retail stores, and restaurants. Feng Shui principles can be particularly beneficial for businesses looking to create positive energy for customers and employees.

How long does the analysis take?

Our AI analysis typically takes 2-5 minutes to process your photos and generate recommendations. For premium plans that include human expert review, you can expect results within 24-48 hours.
